Lasagna

Description

Lasagna is a wide, flat sheet of pasta. Lasagna can refer to either the type of noodle or to the typical lasagna dish which is a dish made with several layers of lasagna sheets with sauce and other ingredients, such as meats and cheese, in between the lasagna noodles

Ingredients

  • Lasagna Noodles
  • Tomato Sauce
  • Ricotta
  • Mozzarella
  • Pecorino

Steps

  1. First thing that goes on the buttered tray is a layer of red sauce.
  2. Add small layer of white sauce
  3. Add layer of pasta
  4. Add layer of sauce and cheese
  5. Repeat process until desired layers reached
