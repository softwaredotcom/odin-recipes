Description

Ground Beef Tacos is one of our favorite weeknight family meals, especially on Taco Tuesdays! I have a quick homemade taco seasoning that I love to use for this recipe so I can cook the taco meat in 10 minutes. Give it a try and itâ€™ll be your new go-to for taco night too!

Ingredients

  • Tortillas
  • Ground Beef
  • Tortillas
  • Onions
  • Cilantro
  • Salsa

Steps

  1. Cook ground beef
  2. Chop and dice onions and cilantro
  3. Heat tortillas over flame
  4. Layer two tortillas ontop of each other
  5. Add cooked ground beed to tortillas
  6. Add diced onions and cilantro to taste
  7. Top off with salsa. Enjoy!
