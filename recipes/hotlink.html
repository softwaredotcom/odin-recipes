Hot Link

Ingredients

  • Hotlink sausage
  • Hot dog buns
  • Jalapenos
  • Mustard
  • Ketchup
  1. Cross-score two sides of hot link.
  2. Slice jalapenos and place on griddle with butter
  3. Cook hotlinks until charred on all sides.
  4. Heat buns on griddle with butter
  5. Assemble hotlink and bun and garnish with jalapenos, mustard and ketchup. Enjoy!
